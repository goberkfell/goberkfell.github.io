The Atlanta Hawks

The Atlanta Hawks have a young roster chocked to the brim with emerging talent and potential stars that could take over the NBA in only a few years.

Trae Young

The shooter extraordinaire and instinctive floor general, Trae Young, has shown in his only college season at Oklahoma as well as the few games this 2018-2019 season that he is capable of making the switch to the pros. Coming out recently with a 35 point & 11 assist game, Young has shown slight sparks of the player he can become with only a few more years under his belt. More of his stats can be viewed here.

John Collins

One of the most athletic forwards coming into the league in the past few years, has shown strides towards becoming a fundamental player that can not only show out with highlight dunks, but also put the ball on the floor and take it to the hole as well as be a threat from behind the three-point line. The tandem that is forming between Young and Collins is electric, as many lobs have been thrown to Collins from Young which have brought the Atlanta crowds to a standing ovation.
